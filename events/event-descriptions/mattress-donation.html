Mattress Donation

The importance of good nap cannot be emphasized more and mattress is a necessity for quality sleep. Upon noticing that many good mattresses are wasted at the end of the year, NSS IITH organizes mattress donation where viable mattress are collected from students and provided to old age homes and orphanages to secure their good sleep. This event is conducted once a year, many graduating students and volunteers contribute to it by donating their mattress.
