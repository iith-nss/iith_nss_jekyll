World Environment Day 2024



Hello everyone,


World Environment Day is celebrated annually on June 5th to promote awareness and action for protecting our environment. The celebration of World Environment Day aims to encourage people to appreciate the planet's natural resources and work towards conserving them for future generations.


This year, we would like to conduct various online events to promote environmental awareness, encourage sustainable practices, and inspire action towards protecting our planet based on this year's theme, Ecosystem Restoration.


Events:

  1. Pledge taking
  2. Hand-Drawn Poster
  3. Tree Planting Photo/Video

Pledge Taking:


As part of our World Environment Day celebrations, we invite you to take a pledge to show your commitment to protecting and restoring our planet. By recording and sharing your pledge, you help inspire others to take action and join the movement for a sustainable future.


Instructions:

  1. Record a short video of at most 2 min of you taking the attached pledge. Drive link to pledge
  2. The video length should strictly be in the specified range.
  3. Shoot the video in good lighting so that you are visible and your voice is audible.
  4. Stand up on your feet, place your right hand on your heart and take the pledge above. Only then will your video entry be considered for evaluation.
  5. Compress the video file, upload it in .mp4 format to your drive, and share it with NSS for view access. Upload the drive link using the Google form.

Hand-Drawn Poster:


Express your creativity and commitment to the environment by participating in our hand-drawn poster event. Create a poster on the given topics to communicate the importance of ecosystem restoration visually.


Topics:

  • The Role of Trees in Ecosystem Restoration.
  • Visualizing the Future: A Restored Planet.

Instructions:

  1. Create a hand-drawn poster on any one of the given topics. We prefer you use an A4 sheet for your drawing to ensure uniformity.
  2. A maximum of 20 words can be used in the poster. Quotes can be used as long as they satisfy the word limit mentioned. Be simple and avoid complex quotes.
  3. A picture can be used from the Internet as a reference for the drawing. If used, please send the links to the image(s).
  4. The poster should contain the logos of NSS and IITH. You can add the IITH and NSS logos after scanning your drawing. It should not contain references to other entities, including student names.
  5. The poster you sent should meet minimum standards. Failing to meet the minimum standards means you will not be awarded any hours.
  6. Upload a scanned copy or a clear photograph of your artwork to your drive and share it with NSS with view access. Upload the file using the provided Google form.

Tree Planting Photo/Video:


Join us in making a tangible impact on the environment by planting a tree. Capture the moment with a photo or video to showcase your contribution to ecosystem restoration and encourage others to do the same.


Instructions:

  1. Plant a tree and document the process with a photo or a short video of 3-5 minutes.
  2. The video length should strictly be in the specified range.
  3. Shoot the video in good lighting and conditions. Ensure the photo/video is clear and the planting action is visible.
  4. Upload the photo/video to your drive and share it with NSS with view access. Upload the drive link using the provided Google form.

Please submit your entries by filling out this form.


Only one file can be uploaded under a question in the above form. Hence, if your entry is not a single file, please upload a zip file.


Only one entry per topic is allowed in each event. In case of multiple entries on a topic, hours will be given to the best one.


You can upload videos, photos and posters directly using the provided form. Make sure to give NSS access to every file you upload.


We will not consider entries mailed to NSS for evaluation. Please submit the entries only through the form.


The best entries will be uploaded on our Instagram handle.


Plagiarism is strictly not tolerated. Any case might lead to negative hours for NSS volunteers.


Deadline: 3rd June 2024, 11:59 PM.


Hours will be given under Category - 2


Any submissions after the deadline will not be considered for evaluation.


World Blood Donor Day 2024



Hello everyone,


World Blood Donor Day is an event celebrated on 14th June every year to raise awareness of the need for safe blood and blood products and to thank voluntary, unpaid blood donors for their life-saving gifts of blood. The campaign slogan for this year's World Blood Donor day is “Celebrating 20 years of giving: thank you, blood donors!” to thank and recognize the millions of voluntary blood donors who have contributed to the health and well-being of millions of people around the world.


On this occasion of World Blood Donor Day, we would like to conduct various online events to promote the values of voluntary unpaid blood donation in enhancing community solidarity and social cohesion.


Events:

  1. Poster Making
  2. Infographics

Poster Making:


Topic: Create a poster on the theme "You're the Heartbeat of Hope: Thank You Blood Donors!" to thank the blood donors.


Instructions:

  1. Make a poster or drawing on any one of the above themes; the poster should be of dimensions 2000 x 2500.
  2. A maximum of 20 words can be used in the poster. Quotes can be used as long as they satisfy the word limit mentioned. Be simple and avoid complex quotes.
  3. The poster should contain the logos of NSS and IITH. Total hours will not be given if the linked NSS logo is unused. It should not contain references to any other entity, including organization watermarks, student names etc.

  7. Violation of any of the rules mentioned above may lead to a reduction of hours for the topic.

International Plastic Bag Free Day 2024



Hello everyone,


International Plastic Bag Free Day is observed on July 3 every year to encourage people to stop using single-use plastic and consequently eliminate the single-use plastic bags with better alternatives.


On this occasion of International Plastic Bag Free Day, we would like to conduct various online events to raise awareness about devastating impacts of single-use plastic bags and to promote alternatives for the single-use plastic bags on the theme "Skip the Bag, Save the Planet: Say No to Single-Use Plastic!"


Events:

  1. Case Study
  2. Poster Making
  3. Dialogue Writing

Case Study:


Topic: "The Ripple Effect: Impact of Single-Use Plastic on Oceans and the Himalayas"


Make sure your case study includes the below objectives.

  1. The differences between normal plastics (multi-use) and single-use plastics.
  2. Environmental and ecological effects of single-use plastics in the oceans and the Himalayas.
  3. The socio-economic implications of plastic pollution in these regions.
  4. Practical, sustainable solutions to reduce and manage single-use plastic waste.

Instructions:

  1. Make a case study on the above topic with a word count of minimum of 500 words.
  2. Upload it either as a Word Document or a PDF.
  3. Plagiarized entries will be given negative hours.
  4. Non-copyable PDFs are not allowed. If found, zero hours will be given.

Poster Making:


Topics:

  1. Alternatives to Single-use Plastics
  2. Plastics in Our Environment: Visualizing its impact


Instructions:

  1. Make a poster or drawing on any one of the above topic(s), the poster should be of dimensions 2000 x 2500.
  2. A maximum of 20 words can be used in the poster. Quotes can be used as long as they satisfy the word limit mentioned. Be simple and avoid complex quotes.
  3. The poster should contain the logos of NSS and IITH. It should not contain references to any other entity, including organization watermarks, student names etc.
  4. A picture can be used from the Internet as long as there are no words in the picture. Please be mindful of the copyright and send the links to the images you use in the poster.
  5. You're requested to send the original file (PSD files or similar) as well the rendered final image (JPG / PNG). You also have to send all the links you used for images, fonts etc.
  6. The poster you sent should meet minimum standards. Failing to meet the minimum standards means that you will not be awarded any hours.
  7. Violation of any of the rules mentioned above may lead to reduction of hours for the topic.

Dialogue Writing:


Topic: "Choose Reusables: Embrace Sustainable Solutions Today"


Description: You saw a friend carrying groceries in the single use plastic-bag.Now write a dialogue between you and your friend that emphasizes the importance of shifting from single-use plastic bags to more sustainable alternatives. It needs to provide a clear problem (the environmental impact of plastic bags) and a practical solution (using reusable bags), aiming to raise awareness and encourage positive behavioral change.


Instructions:

  1. It has to be written with 8-14 exchanges drawing out a conclusion.
  2. You can name the characters on your own and mention them at the top of the script.
  3. Upload it either as Word Document or a PDF. Handwritten entries will not be considered.
  4. The conclusion of the dialogue needs to be mentioned at the end.
  5. Using any abusive/offensive language will lead to immediate disqualification.

Only one entry per topic is allowed in each event. In case of multiple entries on a topic, hours will be given to the best one.
