WORLD BLOOD DONOR DAY 2024

World Blood Donor Day is an event celebrated on 14th June every year to raise awareness of the need for safe blood and blood products and to thank voluntary, unpaid blood donors for their life-saving gifts of blood. The campaign slogan for this year's World Blood Donor day is “Celebrating 20 years of giving: thank you, blood donors!” to thank and recognize the millions of voluntary blood donors who have contributed to the health and well-being of millions of people around the world.

World Blood Donor Day 2024



Hello everyone,


World Blood Donor Day is an event celebrated on 14th June every year to raise awareness of the need for safe blood and blood products and to thank voluntary, unpaid blood donors for their life-saving gifts of blood. The campaign slogan for this year's World Blood Donor day is “Celebrating 20 years of giving: thank you, blood donors!” to thank and recognize the millions of voluntary blood donors who have contributed to the health and well-being of millions of people around the world.


On this occasion of World Blood Donor Day, we would like to conduct various online events to promote the values of voluntary unpaid blood donation in enhancing community solidarity and social cohesion.


Events:

  1. Poster Making
  2. Infographics

Poster Making:


Topic: Create a poster on the theme "You're the Heartbeat of Hope: Thank You Blood Donors!" to thank the blood donors.


Instructions:

  1. Make a poster or drawing on any one of the above themes; the poster should be of dimensions 2000x2500.
  2. A maximum of 20 words can be used in the poster. Quotes can be used as long as they satisfy the word limit mentioned. Be simple and avoid complex quotes.
  3. The poster should contain the logos of NSS and IITH. Total hours will not be given if the linked NSS logo is unused. It should not contain references to any other entity, including organization watermarks, student names etc.
  4. A picture can be used from the Internet if there are no words in the picture. Please be mindful of the copyright and send the links to the images you use in the poster.
  5. You're requested to send the original file (PSD files or similar) as well as the rendered final image (JPG / PNG). You must also send all the links you used for images, fonts etc.
  6. The poster you sent should meet minimum standards. Failing to meet the minimum standards means you will not be awarded any hours.
  7. Violation of any of the rules mentioned above may lead to a reduction of hours for the topic.

Infographics:


Topic: Create an infographic on "Debunking the myths of Blood Donation: Donate with Confidence!">


Instructions:

  1. Illustrate any of the above topic(s) in the infographic within 2-3 pages with a total word limit of 90-120 per page.
  2. It should contain logos of NSS and IITH and no references to any other entity, including organization watermarks, student names, etc.
  3. The aspect ratio must be 1:1 or 4:5, and the format must be .jpg / .png.
  4. Mention the links of all images and fonts that are used.
  5. Do not submit posters for infographics. Those entries will not be considered for evaluation.

Only one entry per topic is allowed in each event. In case of multiple entries on a topic, hours will be given to the best one.


We will not consider entries mailed to NSS for evaluation. Please submit the entries only through the form.


Plagiarism is strictly not tolerated. Any case might lead to negative hours for NSS volunteers.
