International Plastic Bag Free Day 2024



Hello everyone,


International Plastic Bag Free Day is observed on July 3 every year to encourage people to stop using single-use plastic and consequently eliminate the single-use plastic bags with better alternatives.


On this occasion of International Plastic Bag Free Day, we would like to conduct various online events to raise awareness about devastating impacts of single-use plastic bags and to promote alternatives for the single-use plastic bags on the theme "Skip the Bag, Save the Planet: Say No to Single-Use Plastic!"


Events:

  1. Case Study
  2. Poster Making
  3. Dialogue Writing

Case Study:


Topic: "The Ripple Effect: Impact of Single-Use Plastic on Oceans and the Himalayas"


Make sure your case study includes the below objectives.

  1. The differences between normal plastics (multi-use) and single-use plastics.
  2. Environmental and ecological effects of single-use plastics in the oceans and the Himalayas.
  3. The socio-economic implications of plastic pollution in these regions.
  4. Practical, sustainable solutions to reduce and manage single-use plastic waste.

Instructions:

  1. Make a case study on the above topic with a word count of minimum of 500 words.
  2. Upload it either as a Word Document or a PDF.
  3. Plagiarized entries will be given negative hours.
  4. Non-copyable PDFs are not allowed. If found, zero hours will be given.

Poster Making:


Topics:

  1. Alternatives to Single-use Plastics
  2. Plastics in Our Environment: Visualizing its impact


Instructions:

  1. Make a poster or drawing on any one of the above topic(s), the poster should be of dimensions 2000 x 2500.
  2. A maximum of 20 words can be used in the poster. Quotes can be used as long as they satisfy the word limit mentioned. Be simple and avoid complex quotes.
  3. The poster should contain the logos of NSS and IITH. It should not contain references to any other entity, including organization watermarks, student names etc.
  4. A picture can be used from the Internet as long as there are no words in the picture. Please be mindful of the copyright and send the links to the images you use in the poster.
  5. You're requested to send the original file (PSD files or similar) as well the rendered final image (JPG / PNG). You also have to send all the links you used for images, fonts etc.
  6. The poster you sent should meet minimum standards. Failing to meet the minimum standards means that you will not be awarded any hours.
  7. Violation of any of the rules mentioned above may lead to reduction of hours for the topic.

Dialogue Writing:


Topic: "Choose Reusables: Embrace Sustainable Solutions Today"


Description: You saw a friend carrying groceries in the single use plastic-bag.Now write a dialogue between you and your friend that emphasizes the importance of shifting from single-use plastic bags to more sustainable alternatives. It needs to provide a clear problem (the environmental impact of plastic bags) and a practical solution (using reusable bags), aiming to raise awareness and encourage positive behavioral change.


Instructions:

  1. It has to be written with 8-14 exchanges drawing out a conclusion.
  2. You can name the characters on your own and mention them at the top of the script.
  3. Upload it either as Word Document or a PDF. Handwritten entries will not be considered.
  4. The conclusion of the dialogue needs to be mentioned at the end.
  5. Using any abusive/offensive language will lead to immediate disqualification.

Only one entry per topic is allowed in each event. In case of multiple entries on a topic, hours will be given to the best one.


We will not consider entries mailed to NSS for evaluation. We kindly request you submit the entries only through the form.


Plagiarism is strictly not tolerated. Any case might lead to negative hours for NSS volunteers.
