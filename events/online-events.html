International Nature Conservation Day 2024



Hello everyone,


International Nature Conservation Day is observed on July 28 every year to encourage people to understand the importance of preserving the environment and natural resources.


On this occasion of International Nature Conservation Day, we would like to conduct various online events to raise awareness about the vital need for conservation and promote practices that contribute to protecting our planet on the Theme "Protect Nature, Preserve Our Future: Commit to Conservation!"


Events:

  1. Poster Making
  2. Pledge

Poster Making:


Topic: Threats to Marine Ecosystems - Ocean Conservation


Instructions:

  1. Make a poster or drawing on any one of the above themes; the poster should be of dimensions 2000 x 2500.
  2. A maximum of 20 words can be used in the poster. Quotes can be used as long as they satisfy the word limit mentioned. Be simple and avoid complex quotes.
  3. The poster should contain the logos of NSS and IITH. Total hours will not be given if the linked NSS logo is unused. It should not contain references to any other entity, including organization watermarks, student names etc.
  4. A picture can be used from the Internet if there are no words in the picture. Please be mindful of the copyright and send the links to the images you use in the poster.
  5. You're requested to send the original file (PSD files or similar) as well as the rendered final image (JPG / PNG). You must also send all the links you used for images, fonts etc.
  6. The poster you sent should meet minimum standards. Failing to meet the minimum standards means you will not be awarded any hours.
  7. Violation of any of the rules mentioned above may lead to a reduction of hours for the topic.

Pledge:


Topic: Guardians of Nature - Our Commitment to Conservation


Instructions:

  1. Please make sure to record a short video of at most 2 minutes of you taking the attached pledge.Pledge Link
  2. The video length should strictly be in the specified range.
  3. Shoot the video in good lighting so that you are visible and your voice is clear and audible.
  4. Stand up, place your right hand on your heart and take the pledge above. Just so you know, only then will your video entry be considered for evaluation.
  5. Compress the video file, upload it in .mp4 format to your drive, and share it with NSS for view access. Upload the drive link using the Google form.

We will not consider entries mailed to NSS for evaluation. Please submit the entries only through the form.


Plagiarism is strictly not tolerated. Any case might lead to negative hours for NSS volunteers.


Please submit your entries by filling out the form: Form Link


Deadline: 26th July 2024, 11:59 PM


For NSS volunteers, hours will be given under Category - 4


Any submissions after the deadline will not be considered for evaluation.
