Swachh Bharat

Cleanliness may be defined as the emblem of purity of mind. If the vicinities are clean, the atmosphere becomes so fresh and lively. It is the responsibility of every citizen to keep their surroundings clean. Swachhta hi Seva is an event conducted in the campus to ensure that the vicinity is clean and safe. Students attend this event in higher strengths and try their maximum to make the event successful.
