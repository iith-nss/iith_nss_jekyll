Vidyadaan

Vidyadaan is an attempt to share knowledge with the children of government schools, which will be conducted regularly. The main motto is to ensure that the children don't lack basic skills like self-introduction in English, simple math, etc. We conduct a few activities to teach them concepts instead of regular teaching. We also conduct games as an extra activity to cheer them up. This event also acts as a stress-reliever for volunteers who get a chance to spend time with children.
